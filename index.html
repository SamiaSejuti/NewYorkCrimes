Crimes can't keep tourists away

Despite the crimes committed in public, the city attracts tourists in flocks. The tourist attractions are more prone to crime. The Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Empire State Building, 9/11 Memorial Museum, etc are amongst the top.

In the five boroughs of NYC, MISDEMEANOR encompasses the top crimes like petit larceny, criminal mischief, assault, unauthorized use of vehicle and public order offenses. FELONY includes robbery, grand larceny, violation of laws and assault, while a major part of VIOLATION consists of harrassment.

Let's take a look at the stats over the years

The stats showed were only for the holiday season when the crime rate increases significantly. Manhattan, being the most popular borough with the most number of tourist spots, has got the most number of crimes. The crimes are peak from the afternoon till dark leaving people alone in the foreign city. Over the number of years these crimes do not seem to be decreasing.

Come, let's altogether take the step to reduce the crime, protect the people, protect the city, and protect the tourism industry.
